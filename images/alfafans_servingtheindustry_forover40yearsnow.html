Alfa Fans were established in 1981 and have been growing ever since. Alfa Fans is a Leading Manufacturer , Wholesaler and Exporter Of High Quality Heavy Duty Industrial Fans In Mumbai, India. Our belief runs in long-term customer association and manufacturing fans based on individual customer needs and requirements. We are customer oriented and our company is driven by its commitment to quality.
Alfa Fans is one of the highly acclaimed Industrial Centrifugal Air Blowers and Heavy Duty Tube Axial Fans manufacturer from India. The company is driven by engineers with sound technical knowledge, and blend of vast experience in the field of Industrial Air Blowers.



We take efforts in understanding the customer’s requirement and offer them the right air blower. All Centrifugal air blowers & Tube axial fans the company offers are well designed, critically analyzed and tested for heavy duty operation. Special Care is taken during design and manufacturing stages to increase efficiency and reliability of our centrifugal blowers and fans. Our capability in offering superior technology at competitive price differs us from the rest of the manufacturers of Industrial Centrifugal Blowers and Axial fans.



Quality is achieved by proper inspection checks right from material purchasing to final testing of air blowers. Our entire range of centrifugal air blowers & tube axial fan have relevant test certificates provided at the time of final inspection and testing of centrifugal blower.



Our focus is to develop and continuously upgrade technology for thereby creating efficient and reliable products which will satisfy our customers needs to the fullest. Over the years we have gained specialization in designing and manufacturing of the centrifugal blowers and tube axial fan to suit the application and environment in which your air blower will be working.



Our mission is to be a world leader in manufacturing premium-quality, engineered fans and blowers to the industrial marketplace; fulfilling the need for reliable air movement and timely delivery.
Through a continuous effort to improve quality and adherence to the highest ethical standards.

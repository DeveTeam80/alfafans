What is an air circulator fan?
Air Circulators are specially designed to throw air at about a length of 10 meters . The aerodynamically designed blades ensure higher air delivery and minimum noise level. The sturdy guards with powder coating ensure corrosion resistance as per safety standards.
What is a close mesh fan?
Close Mesh Fans are new technology innovation, these are industrial fans designed with heavier duty materials and components to operate longer, supply greater air flow and pressure, and meet more stringent environmental and ambient requirements.
What are the Benefits of using close mesh fans?
It is 100 % Finger Cut protection from the Rotating Part. Non corrosive & designed to work in all the season Weather and chemical resistant. It has a unique design & polymer property and is flexible & strong with no Air Obstruction Durable & Light weight. Easy and Quick fixing.

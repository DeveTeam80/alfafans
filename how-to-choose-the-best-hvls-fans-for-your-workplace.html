How to choose the best HVLS fans for your workplace

  • Oct,2022
  • Alfa Fans
  • BLOG

HVLS fans, or

high volume low speed fans

, are an evolution of the basic industrial ceiling fan. HVLS fan blades were inspired by airplane wings and are uniquely engineered to transport enormous volumes of air uniformly from the center hub to the blade tip. Most manufacturers offer HVLS fans in a range of diameters ranging from 8' to 24' in diameter. Despite their size, most HVLS fan motors produce less than 2 horsepower. As a result, they are a low-cost way of moving large volumes of air.

After extensive research and deliberation, you've concluded that a high volume low speed (HVLS) fan is the best option for cooling your workplace. Your work, however, is far from finished. You must also choose the proper fan. A variety of factors influence the type of fan you use at your workplace. Here are some questions to ask yourself when deciding which HVLS fan is best for your room.

If you've decided to purchase an HVLS fan for your home or business, there are a few things you should consider before having it installed. For example, you may be unsure of how many fans you will require for your room, how large they should be, or where they should be placed.

How Much Space Is Required for HVLS Fan Cooling?

Logistics centers require a high level of speed and pacing. This type of activity and traffic generates heat in a variety of environments. This heat buildup is especially noticeable during the hot summer months, when logistic facilities have open bays and loading docks that allow heat to enter the workplace. Using HVLS fans, warehouse and logistic facilities can de-stratify the air and combine the heat strata that are building up from the ceiling down. A floor jet circulation provides significant cooling and eliminates trapped heat compartments that can be found in massive buildings.

In general, the larger the area to be cooled, the larger the fan needed. Most manufacturers sell HVLS fans in a variety of sizes, which you can find on ALFA Fans, with each one designed to cool a specific range of spaces. For example, if you have limited space, you may only need a 12′ fan. A fan between 18′ and 24′ tall is required to cool a larger space, such as a warehouse,distribution center or manufacturing areas. The size of the space to be cooled is directly proportional to the type of fan you select for your workplace.

HVLS fan price in India

  • Size: The price of HVLS fans can differ based on the size you select before purchasing.
  • Costs of Maintenance and Repair: Most fans require a certain amount of maintenance to keep them in good working order.
  • When selecting an HVLS fan, consider all of these factors, not just the price.

Alfa Fans is located in Mumbai, India. Our company was established in 1981 and specialized in producing industrial fans and Blowers. We successfully developed , design and manufacture industrial Pedastal Fans , Wall Mount Fans , Exhaust Fans , Tubular Fans , Axial Flow Fans , Man Coolers , Centrifugal Blowers and more. with the advanced technology. We are Leading Manufacturer , Wholesaler and Exporter Of High Quality Heavy Duty Industrial Fans.


Our team are dedicated to solve industrial workshop, warehouse, public places and other large spaces for personnel cooling, air circulation, smoke dust problems. We sincerely welcome the clients from home and abroad. And we will try our best to supply you with high quality products and our best service to your entire satisfaction. This outstanding integrity and reliability is highly appreciated by our customers and clearly set us apart from the competition. We hope to advance hand in hand with you and create glorious future together!
